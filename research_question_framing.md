# Provisional Lane

Content Refresh Prioritization

---

# Research Question

Which web pages should be refreshed first to improve search visibility and organic traffic?

---

# Unit of Analysis

Individual web pages.

---

# Expected Output

A ranked list of pages that should be updated first.

---

# Action

SEO teams will update the highest priority pages before others.

---

# Cost of a Wrong Recommendation

If the wrong pages are selected:

- Time will be wasted.
- Traffic may continue to decrease.
- Important pages could be ignored.
- Business revenue may be affected.

---

# Why Machine Learning?

Machine Learning can analyze many signals such as impressions, clicks, CTR, page position, and historical trends to identify pages that need refreshing better than simple manual rules.

---

# Conclusion

This research question aims to help SEO teams make better decisions by prioritizing pages that are most likely to benefit from content updates.
